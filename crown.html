Crown of Red

Coffee, community, culture. Crown of Red is a coffee shop serving for the people. An artisan coffee shop without the air of superiority. It is meant to be a place of social gathering where friends can have a good time and the communicty can come together for a greater purpose.

Fonts and Colors

Raleway was chosen as the primary font as it has an art deco, vintage look but is still modern. Raleway will primarily be used for titles and headers while Montserrat is used for sub headings and bodies of text. Montserrat is an easy-to-read sans-serif font which makes it great for larger bodies of text.

Logo and Identity

Hummingbirds are adored by many. They are a hard workers and have tons of energy which makes it a great representation for Crown of Red. In Native American folklore, hummingbirds were a symbol of healing which remains consistent with Crown of Red's mission to serve the community.
