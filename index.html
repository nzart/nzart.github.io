About

UI Designer, coffee afficionado, runner, and expert cookie maker.

I studied Graphic Communications at Clemson University. And during my time there, I focused on UI design and development as my personal track. I enjoyed learning the ins and outs of HTML, and CSS and being able to make visual pleasing sites. Don't get me wrong, early on they were horrendous but I still enjoyed learning front end development. I am currently learning React as well as rebuilding this site using Gatsby.

Thanks to Graphic Communications, I was able to intern with two great companies; one outside of Atlanta, GA and the other in Memphis, TN. In Memphis, I had the opportunity to work on a special project and help in the redesign of the companies website. Unfortunately, my department had other obligations so I couldn't work on it full time. But that special project kept reminding me of why I love web design.

Other than web design, I enjoy coffee, running and baking. Running has given me an outlet during stressful times as well as given me the opportunity to meet some of my closest friends. I love visiting new coffee shops when I travel. Each one has a unique charm that inspires me in different ways. My go to is a cortado. And as for my cookie making, I've had moms ask me for my recipe. If that's not a compliment, I don't know what is!
