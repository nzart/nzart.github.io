About Me
Hello! My name is Nick Zart and I’m from Jacksonville, FL. I am a Senior studying Graphic Communications at Clemson University. I’m most likely learning, running, or perfecting the pour over. I am an avid Liverpool fan as well. Oh and I can make some of the best cookies in the world.
Education
I have been extrememly fortunate during my time at Clemson and I have had the opportunity to meet great people. I took an introductory web design and development class my sophomore year and that really set the tone for what career I wanted to pursue. I have spent many hours learning as much as I can through Udemy, FreeCodeCamp, and of course through my professors. I am currently learning React and I have enjoyed the process so far. I believe that being a life-long learner is extremely important, and not only in web design and development.
Work
One of the reasons I recommend Graphic Communications is because you are required to have two internships to graduate. I was able to intern with two great companies, one being outside Atlanta, GA and the other was in Memphis, TN. Throughout both internships I truly realized the importance of communication. And of course both gave me the oppurtunity to work in a team where everyone had a different role and I loved seeing everything come together.
Front End & UX
As mentioned, since Sophomore year I have been devoted to learning development and design. Having a background in print gives me a unique view of what works and why. I have also been able to use my experience to understand that different people and industries have different needs. I am excited for what is to come. I am more than ready to join a team and really push myself. The only way to get better is to get uncomfortable right?
