Working with TigerVision
About
I was asked to do a design for a show on our student run television station, TigerVision. The show was a late night talk show and that is where a lot of the inspiration for the design came from. Being a Clemson University station themes from the campus came in to play as well. I was initially asked to include library bridge in to the design but I created a design featuring a silhouette of Old Main which was featured in the final, chosen design. While the show did not last long, I enjoyed coming up with a design as well as working with the host.

Nick Zart
