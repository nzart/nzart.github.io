About
While I am pursuing development more than design, I wanted to inlcude one of my earliest, and most favorite, designs. The objective was to create a two color design as well as formulate a brand. I was inspired by NYCFC's first away kits, more specifically a picture of Pirlo and Villa celebrating a goal, and choosing the blue and orange of New York City's flag as my two colors. I chose Helvetica as the font because it is easy to read as well as being the primary font of New York's MTA. The outline features the Empire State Building as well as the One World Trade building.
